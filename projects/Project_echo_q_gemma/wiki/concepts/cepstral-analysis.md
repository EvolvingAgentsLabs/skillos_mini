Applying $\\log$ yields $\\log|P(\\omega)| + \\log|1 + \\alpha e^{-i\\omega\\tau}|$.\nQED: The second term is periodic in frequency, which appears as a peak at quefrency $\\tau$ in the cepstrum.\n\n## Related Concepts\n- [[homomorphic-signal-separation]] — The math behind the log-transform.\n- [[quantum-fourier-transform]] — The quantum tool for the FFT steps.\n\n## Open Questions\n- Robustness of quantum cepstrum against high noise levels.\n
