LLM-OS games (raw demos)

Both demos run on the same kernel as llm_os. They download a 230 MB local model on first load, then play entirely in the browser via WebAssembly.

These URLs are the raw demos with no strategy injection. To pick a strategy, go to the app launcher and click a strategy card — it appends ?strategy=<id> to the URL, the demo fetches the strategy markdown, and prepends it to the system prompt before model load.

▶ Tetris

A 350M-parameter LLM plays Tetris. The Program layer enumerates every legal placement (4 rotations × 10 columns), simulates the post-placement metrics, scores them with Dellacherie-style weights, and surfaces the top 3 as best_actions. The LLM picks one and emits its opcode sequence.

cartridge: game/tetris · 4 methods · pre-ranked top-3 placements

▶ Scavenger

A 350M-parameter LLM solves a multi-step quest. Find the red cube, deliver to the blue square — through walls, around pits, with a 30-move budget. The Program layer runs BFS over the grid and surfaces next_step: the first move of the shortest path. The LLM ratifies a single direction.

cartridge: game/scavenger · 4 methods · BFS-pre-computed next move
